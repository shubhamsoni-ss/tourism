Rock Garden of Chandigarh

The Rock Garden of Chandigarh is a sculpture garden in Chandigarh,
 India. It is also known as Nek Chand's Rock Garden after its founder
 Nek Chand, a government official who started the garden secretly in 
his spare time in 1957. Today it is spread over an area of 40 acres. 
Wikipedia
Did you know: Nek Chand was given a salary, a title ("Sub-Divisional 
Engineer, Rock Garden"), and 50 laborers so that he could concentra
-te full-time on his work. 
	
Sukhna Lake
Reservoir in India
4.53,421 Google reviews
Sukhna Lake in Chandigarh, India, is a reservoir at the foothills of the Himalayas. 
This 3 km² rainfed lake was created in 1958 by damming the Sukhna Choe, a sea
-sonal stream coming down from the Shivalik Hills. Originally the seasonal flow 
entered the lake directly causing heavy siltation.
 Wikipedia
Area: 3 km²
Length: 1.52 km
Catchment area: 42.07 km²
Max. depth: 16 ft (4.9 m)
Cities: Chandigarh
Did you know: Sukhna Lake has facilities of boating, rowing, sculling, sailing, kayaking and water skiing that can be enjoyed throughout the year.
